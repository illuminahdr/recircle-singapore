Smart Recycling for a Low‑Waste City

Convenient, rewarding, and trusted recycling: place – scan – earn – verify.

Get Started

Smart Kiosks

Deposit bottles, cans and clean plastics at walkable kiosks. Each item is weighed and scanned to issue verified credits. Our kiosks are convenient, accessible and ensure that every deposit counts toward your sustainability journey.

Track Your Credits

Sign in to view your deposit history, collect points and compete on leaderboards. Challenges and badges keep recycling fun and encourage friendly competition among the community. Stay motivated as you see your positive impact grow.

Redeem Rewards

Exchange your points for transport vouchers, retail discounts and community perks. We partner with local businesses and agencies to offer meaningful rewards that make your recycling efforts even more worthwhile. Everything is verified and fraud‑free.

Our Vision

ReCircle Singapore envisions a city where recycling is effortless, rewarding and trustworthy. By combining smart kiosks with an intuitive digital platform, we make sustainability part of your everyday routine.

Rewards & Partnerships

Earn points for every bottle, can or piece of clean plastic you recycle. Redeem your credits for transport vouchers, retail discounts and community experiences. We work with local businesses and agencies to bring you the best perks as you help the planet.
