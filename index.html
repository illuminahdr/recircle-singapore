How It Works

Smart Kiosks

Deposit bottles, cans and clean plastics at walkable kiosks. Each item is weighed and scanned to issue verified credits.

Track Your Credits

Sign in to view your deposit history, collect points and compete on leaderboards. Challenges and badges keep recycling fun.

Redeem Rewards

Exchange your points for transport vouchers, retail discounts and community perks. Rewards are verified and fraud‑free.

Our Pilot Impact

5 000+ registered users
